My journey began with a curiosity for human connection, leading to a degree in Mass Communication and a career in HR and Communications. But at my core, I am a "Bookrovert"—someone who finds the world most vibrant within the pages of a story. I take the complexities of life—mental health, societal ironies, and the innocence of childhood—and translate them into accessible, moving literature.

My professional copy is fueled by this same empathy; I don’t just write for "customers," I write for "characters" with real motivations. By utilizing "Narrative Decryption," I ensure every piece of content has a compelling narrative arc. Whether exploring the female perspective in poetry or crafting digital strategies for mission-driven brands, my goal is to challenge societal norms and open minds to new ways of thinking.
